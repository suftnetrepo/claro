Privacy Policy

Last updated: 14 May 2026

Claro Budget is a personal finance and budgeting application developed by Suftnet. This Privacy Policy explains how the app handles information when you use Claro Budget.

Information We Collect

Claro Budget is designed to store your budgeting and financial data locally on your device. The app may store information such as accounts, budgets, categories, transactions, and app preferences.

Local Data Storage

Your financial records are stored on your device. We do not sell your personal data and we do not use your budgeting information for advertising.

Premium Subscriptions

Claro Budget offers optional premium features using Apple In-App Purchases. Subscription and purchase processing is handled by Apple and RevenueCat. We do not directly collect or store your payment card details.

Analytics and Diagnostics

The app may use basic diagnostics or purchase status information to help maintain premium access and improve reliability. This information is used only to support app functionality.

Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. Information may only be shared with service providers necessary to operate app functionality, such as Apple for purchases and RevenueCat for subscription entitlement management.

Security

Claro Budget may use device security features such as PIN protection or biometric authentication where available. You are responsible for keeping your device secure.

Children’s Privacy

Claro Budget is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Your Choices

You can delete app data by removing the app from your device. Subscriptions can be managed or cancelled through your Apple ID subscription settings.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Updates will be posted on this page with a revised date.
